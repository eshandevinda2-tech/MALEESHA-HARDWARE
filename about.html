Since 2010, Maleesha Hardware has been the trusted partner for builders, homeowners, and contractors across Sri Lanka.

Who We Are

Maleesha Hardware was founded with a singular vision: to provide high-quality construction materials and tools at affordable prices. What started as a small shop has now grown into a premier hardware supplier in the region.

We pride ourselves on our deep industry knowledge and our commitment to customer satisfaction. Whether you are building a skyscraper or renovating your kitchen, we have the expertise and products to help you succeed.
